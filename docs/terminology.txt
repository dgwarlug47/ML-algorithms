trials: stands for training and evaluating with the same hyper_parameters.

multiple trials: several trials changing the hyper_parameters, and then repeating with the same hyper_parameters.

comments: starting with '#' refer to comments related to the dimensions of the variables, inputs of methods, what the method returns or possible commands that
were turned off, but may be turned on later. starting with ' """ ' refer to what a method is doing at that moment.

aggregates: information aggregated during the trial.

cums: information accumulated from multiple trials.
